About

We wire up school compounds and put them on a dashboard.

Schooled Automation is the hardware-and-software arm of the Schooled family. The sister products handle finance, timetabling, transport, and hostels. We handle the physical compound — every gate, door, light, meter, bell, and cooker.

Local

Installers who know Kenyan dorm wings. Hardware rated for the grid voltage and water pressure you actually have. Invoices in shillings.

All-in-one

We provide the hardware, do the install, and run the dashboard. One agreement, one number to call if something stops responding.

Pilot-first

We start with one block for one term. Three systems connected. At the end you see the report and decide whether to expand.
